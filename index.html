📜 Quest Rules & Rewards

👟 Scoring

Stomp mushroom +5 pts · Stone hit +10 pts · Stomp Elite +10 pts · Stone Elite +20 pts · Every 100 pts: 🎉 milestone chat bubble!

🔥 The Elite Hunt

Stomp 20 regular mushrooms to summon a legendary Elite 🌟. A chat bubble and musical fanfare announce its arrival. Worth double points!

🪙 Coins & Stones

Collect coins on platforms · Buy 10 🪨 for 100 🪙 using the buy button · Throw stones with the throw button · Grab power-ups 🪨 for +5 free stones

🏆 Achievement Milestones

Each unlocks a ⭐ notification banner and +50 coins reward:
🍄 Mushroom Hunter — every 25 stomps  ·  💰 Treasure Seeker — every 200 coins  ·  🪨 Stone Slinger — every 50 stones thrown

📊 Progress Bars

Bottom-right corner shows 3 bars toward your next milestone — ■ Stomper (25 stomps)  ·  ■ Treasure (200 coins)  ·  ■ Slinger (50 stones). They glow when close!

🌈 Environment Shifts

Every 50 enemies stomped transitions the world color palette — shimmer sound + new theme. Toggle in ⚙️ Settings.
